Realm Cards: Heroes - Account Deletion

This page explains how users can delete their Realm Cards: Heroes account and associated data.

Account deletion is available in the Settings section of Realm Cards: Heroes.

Open the app, go to Settings, and use the account deletion option to request deletion of your account and associated data.

How to Request Account Deletion

You can request account deletion in one of the following ways:

  1. Open Realm Cards: Heroes.
  2. Go to Settings.
  3. Select the Delete Account or account deletion option.
  4. Follow the instructions shown in the app.

If you need help, you can contact us by email:

novexustech@gmail.com

Please include the email address used for your Realm Cards: Heroes account and clearly state that you want your account deleted.

What Data May Be Deleted

When your account deletion request is processed, the following data may be deleted where applicable:

  • Account information
  • Login-related account identifier
  • Player profile data
  • Game progress
  • Deck and card-related gameplay data
  • Reward and in-game balance records
  • Gameplay-related records associated with your account

Data That May Be Retained

Some data may be retained if required for legal, security, fraud prevention, financial, tax, dispute resolution, or compliance purposes.

For example, records related to purchases, refunds, fraud prevention, platform compliance, or security logs may need to be retained for a limited period.

Processing Time

We aim to process valid account deletion requests within 30 days.

Partial Data Deletion

If you want to request deletion of specific data without deleting your full account, you may contact us at:

novexustech@gmail.com

Please describe which data you want deleted.
